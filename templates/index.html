Visualizing Housing Market Trends: An Analysis of Sale Prices and Features using Tableau

This project focuses on analyzing real estate data to uncover key insights about property valuations and distribution.

  • The goal is to address challenges in understanding the factors that influence house prices and sales trends.
  • By analyzing comprehensive housing data, including the total sales by years since renovation, house age, and spatial metrics.
  • Utilizing Tableau for this analysis, the objective is to visualize and interpret patterns in the housing market.
